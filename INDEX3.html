Shortbread Cookies

Description

Shortbread cookies are a classic treat known for their buttery, crumbly texture and rich flavor. These cookies are simple to make and perfect for any occasion. Whether enjoyed with a cup of tea or as a sweet snack, shortbread cookies are always a delightful choice.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Gradually add the flour and salt, mixing until the dough comes together.
  4. If using, mix in the vanilla extract.
  5. Roll the dough out on a lightly floured surface to about 1/2 inch thickness.
  6. Cut into desired shapes using cookie cutters.
  7. Place the cookies on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden.
  9. Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
