Grilled Stuffed Poblanos

Description

Fire up dinnertime with these grilled vegetarian stuffed poblano peppers that benefit from charred, smoky flavors.

Ingredients

  • 8 poblano peppers
  • 5 ears corn, husks and silks removed
  • 1 (15-ounce can) no-salt-added black beans
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh oregano
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup shredded pepper Jack cheese
  • 1 cup pico de gallo
  • cilantro leaves, lime wedges
  • chili powder, for garnish

Steps

  1. Preheat to medium-high heat (375 to 400 degrees F (180 to 200 degrees C)

  3. Transfer poblanos to a large bowl, cover, and let stand for 10 minutes.
  4. Remove and discard poblano skins, keeping stems intact
